Nikhil Institutes of Technology

Nikhil Institutes of Technology (NIT):are a group of higher education engineering institutes in Andhra Pradesh. this is located in major district East Godavari. On their inception decades ago, This NIT were referred to as best Engineering Colleges.

NIT offer degree courses at bachelor, masters and doctorate levels in various branches of engineering and technology. Various nationwide college surveys rate most of the NITs over other colleges in India

The admissions to the Undergraduate Programmes at these institutions for all Indian and Foreign nationals are made through the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE)Main.
